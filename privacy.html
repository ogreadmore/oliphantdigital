8. Your Rights

Subject to local law, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you;
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information;
  • Delete or erase your data;
  • Restrict or object to certain processing;

To exercise any right, email privacy@oliphantdigital.com. We will respond within 30 days and may request verification of your identity.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 in the EEA/UK, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have done so, please contact us immediately for removal.

10. International Data Transfers

If you access the Site from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to, stored, or processed in the U.S., where our servers and third‑party processors are located. We protect such transfers through providers certified under the EU‑U.S. Data Privacy Framework and/or by executing Standard Contractual Clauses and conducting Transfer Impact Assessments, as applicable.
