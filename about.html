Frequently Asked Questions

Who’s behind Oliphant Digital?
Oliphant Digital was founded by Taylor Oliphant — a strategist, developer, and business leader with over 20 years of experience helping organizations scale with clarity and confidence. He holds a 4.0 MBA from Purdue University and executive certifications in Artificial Intelligence (University of Pennsylvania) and Leadership (Harvard).

Taylor’s background combines deep business insight with hands-on technical expertise, enabling him to bridge strategy and execution seamlessly. Whether you're looking for big-picture planning or fine-tuned implementation, he’s known for delivering both — with clarity, consistency, and zero drama.

View full resume
How long have you been in business?
Oliphant Digital is built on over two decades of experience. We've been helping businesses grow online since 2001 — long before social media and smartphones reshaped the landscape. That history means we’ve seen what works, what doesn’t, and how to adapt through every shift.
What kinds of businesses do you work with?
We primarily work with small and mid-size businesses across a range of industries — from professional services and tech to consumer goods and nonprofits. If you're growth-minded and looking to level up, we’re built for you.
Do I need to sign a long-term contract?
Nope. We keep things flexible. Most of our work is project-based, with clear timelines and deliverables. That means no bloated retainers, no unclear commitments — just great work, done well.
What makes you different from hiring multiple agencies or freelancers?
We're a true one-stop shop. You won’t need to manage five different teams or spend time getting everyone aligned. From branding and digital marketing to systems design and backend development — it’s all handled by a single, strategic team that’s already in sync.
Can you work with my existing team?
Absolutely. Whether we’re plugging into your internal team or other outside collaborators, we’re used to working cross-functionally. We bring clarity, structure, and a collaborative mindset to every project.
What if I’m not sure what I need yet?
That’s exactly where we come in. We help you assess what’s working, where the gaps are, and what will move the needle. Whether you need strategy, execution, or both — we’ll help clarify the next best step.
